- **Goals**: Investasi aman dengan return optimal, fractional investing

#### Section 1: Investment Strategy Deep Dive
**H2:** "Strategi Investasi: Gold-Equity Rotation"

**Subsection 1.1: The Core Concept**
**Visual:** Interactive diagram showing rotation between gold and equity

**Explanation:**
"Gold Plus Fund menggunakan strategi rotasi dinamis antara dua asset class yang memiliki korelasi mendekati nol dalam jangka panjang:"

**Two-Column Comparison:**

**Column 1: Global Equities**
- Representative Index: MSCI World Index
- Characteristics: Growth-oriented, higher volatility
- Correlation with Gold: ~0 (25-year historical)
- Role: Capture upside during equity bull markets

**Column 2: Physical Gold**
- Representative: Gold bullion (London fixing)
- Characteristics: Defensive, lower volatility
- Correlation with Equities: ~0 (25-year historical)
- Role: Capital preservation during market stress

**Key Insight Box:**
"Dalam 25 tahun terakhir, gold mengalami negative 1-year returns ~30% of the time. Namun, simultaneous drawdowns di BOTH gold DAN equity hanya terjadi ~7% of instances — ini yang membuat kombinasi keduanya powerful."

---

**Subsection 1.2: Allocation Rules (Quantitative Approach)**
**H3:** "3 Skenario Alokasi Otomatis"

**Interactive Scenario Cards:**

**Scenario 1: Both Assets Trending Up ↗️↗️**
- Condition: Gold dan Equity sama-sama menunjukkan positive trend signals
- Action: Alokasikan 100% ke asset dengan **stronger trend signal**
- Rationale: Maximize return dengan fokus pada winner terkuat
- Example: "Jika Equity momentum > Gold momentum → 100% Equity"

**Scenario 2: Only One Asset Trending Up ↗️➡️**
- Condition: Hanya satu asset yang menunjukkan positive trend
- Action: Alokasikan 100% ke asset yang trending
- Rationale: Ride the trend, hindari asset yang sideways/down
- Example: "Jika hanya Gold trending up → 100% Gold"

**Scenario 3: No Positive Trends ➡️⬇️**
- Condition: Tidak ada positive trends (random/bearish market)
- Action: Alokasikan 100% ke asset dengan **lower realized volatility**
- Rationale: Capital preservation mode — pilih asset paling stabil
- Example: "Biasanya → 100% Gold (lower vol)"

**Visual Aid:**
- Decision tree flowchart showing the logic
- Historical allocation chart showing switches over time

---

**Subsection 1.3: Risk Management — Volatility Targeting**
**H3:** "Kontrol Risiko: 10% Annualized Volatility Target"

**Explanation:**
"Setiap alokasi dikalibrasi untuk mempertahankan 10% annualized volatility target, memberikan consistent risk-adjusted experience."

**How It Works:**
- "Jika alokasi 100% equity terlalu volatile → reduce exposure via leverage adjustment"
- "Jika alokasi 100% gold terlalu rendah volatility → may slightly leverage (if allowed)"
- "Target: Memberikan return optimal dengan volatility yang terkontrol"

**Chart:** Historical volatility chart showing consistency around 10% target

**Subsection 2.2: NAV-Based Pricing Mechanism**
**H3:** "Harga Token Selalu Mengikuti NAV Fund"

**Key Principle:**
"GLDN token price is ALWAYS anchored to the NAV per Unit of the underlying Discretionary Mandate, as calculated daily by STAR AM."

**Why This Matters:**
- ✅ No speculative premium/discount
- ✅ True economic value reflection
- ✅ No secondary market manipulation risk
- ✅ Fair pricing for all investors (early and late)

**Pricing Formula:**
```
GLDN Token Price = NAV per Unit (end-of-day)
```

**Example Calculation:**
- NAV per Unit today: $100.50
- Your purchase: 10 GLDN tokens
- Total cost: $1,005 (plus fees)

**NAV Update Schedule:**
- Calculation Frequency: Daily (after market close)
- Publication Time: T+1 (next business day morning)
- Price Effective: For all transactions on day T

**Subsection 2.3: Mint & Redemption Flow**
**H3:** "Cara Beli (Mint) dan Jual (Redeem) GLDN Token"

**Tab 1: SUBSCRIPTION (Minting) FLOW**

**Step-by-Step Visual Timeline:**

**Step 1: User Submits Order** 🛒
- Platform: Via licensed Crypto Exchange atau PT. Sejahtera Bersama Nano portal
- Payment: Fiat (IDR/USD) atau Stablecoin (USDT/USDC)
- Amount: Specify amount to invest

**Step 2: Capital Channeled to KPD** 💰
- Action: PT. Sejahtera Bersama Nano channels capital into KPD via STAR AM
- Timing: Next available NAV calculation date (T+1)
- Process: STAR AM invests according to current allocation rules

**Step 3: NAV Confirmation** 📊
- Action: STAR AM calculates NAV per Unit for day T
- Trigger: NAV confirmation triggers mint instruction
- Calculation: Number of tokens = Investment Amount / NAV per Unit

**Step 4: Token Issuance** 🪙
- Action: GLDN tokens minted on BNB Smart Chain
- Delivery: Tokens delivered to user's wallet address
- Confirmation: On-chain transaction recorded on BSCScan

**Timeline:** Total process ~1-2 business days

---

**Tab 2: REDEMPTION (Burning) FLOW**

**Step-by-Step Visual Timeline:**

**Step 1: User Submits Redemption Request** 📤
- Platform: Via licensed Crypto Exchange
- Specify: Number of GLDN tokens to redeem
- Initiation: Submit redemption request

**Step 2: Redemption Processing** 🔄
- Action: PT. Sejahtera Bersama Nano processes redemption from KPD
- Timing: Next end-of-day NAV (T+1)
- STAR AM: Liquidates corresponding position in fund

**Step 3: Token Burning** 🔥
- Action: GLDN tokens burned on BNB Smart Chain
- Trigger: Upon settlement confirmation from STAR AM
- Verification: Burn transaction recorded on-chain

**Step 4: Proceeds Returned** 💵
- Action: Proceeds returned to user
- Format: Fiat (IDR/USD) atau Stablecoin per user preference
- Calculation: Redemption Amount = Tokens × NAV per Unit (minus fees)

**Timeline:** Total process ~2-3 business days

**Note:** Large redemptions may be subject to reasonable queuing per KPD liquidity terms.

- **Focus Indicators:** Clear focus states for keyboard users
- **Form Labels:** All inputs have associated labels
- **Error Handling:** Clear, descriptive error messages

## 🧠 Information Architecture Principles Applied

### 1. **User-Centered Organization**
- Content grouped by user goals, not internal structure
- "Invest Now" prominently featured (primary user goal)
- "Support" easily accessible (user need)

### 2. **Clear Labeling**
- Navigation uses plain language (not jargon)
- "How It Works" instead of "Mechanism"
- "Performance" instead of "Analytics"

### 3. **Shallow Hierarchy**
- Max 3 levels deep (Home → Category → Detail)
- Important info within 2 clicks

### 4. **Multiple Paths to Content**
- Key info accessible from multiple entry points
- Whitepaper downloadable from Home, About, Resources
- Contact options on every page (footer)

### 5. **Progressive Disclosure**
- Simple overview on Home
- Deeper details on dedicated pages
- Expandable sections (accordions) for advanced info

### 6. **Consistent Navigation**
- Global nav always visible
- Breadcrumbs on deep pages
- "Back to Top" button on long pages

### 7. **Search & Findability**
- Site-wide search
- FAQ search
- Clear page titles and meta descriptions for SEO

### 8. **Context-Sensitive Help**
- Tooltips on complex terms
- "Learn more" links to educational content
- Contextual CTAs (e.g., "Need help? Chat with us")
